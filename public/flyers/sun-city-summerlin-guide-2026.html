Championship Golf Courses

Sun City Summerlin is a golfer's paradise with four distinct courses, each offering unique challenges and breathtaking views of the Las Vegas valley and Spring Mountains.

Highland Falls Golf Club

Par 72 | 6,512 yards | Designed by Billy Casper & Greg Nash

The crown jewel of Sun City, featuring dramatic elevation changes and stunning mountain views. Known for its challenging layout and impeccable conditioning.

Palm Valley Golf Club

Par 72 | 6,580 yards | Designed by Greg Nash

A desert-style course with wide fairways and strategic bunkering. Perfect for players of all skill levels seeking a fair but engaging round.

Eagle Crest Golf Club

Par 60 | 3,525 yards | Executive Course

An executive-length course ideal for a quick round or honing your short game. Features challenging par-3s and par-4s in a scenic setting.

Starbright Par 3 Course

Par 27 | 9 Holes | Lighted for Night Play

A unique par-3 course with night lighting, allowing residents to play under the stars. Perfect for practice or casual evening rounds.

Nevada Tax Benefits for Retirees

Nevada is one of the most tax-friendly states for retirees in the nation. Moving to Sun City Summerlin could save you thousands of dollars annually compared to high-tax states like California.

Major Tax Advantages

✓
No State Income Tax

Keep more of your retirement income, Social Security, pensions, and investment earnings.

✓
No Inheritance Tax

Pass your assets to your heirs without state inheritance taxes.

✓
No Estate Tax

Your estate won't be subject to state estate taxes.

✓
No Tax on Social Security

100% of your Social Security benefits are tax-free at the state level.

✓
No Tax on Retirement Income

Pensions, 401(k), IRA distributions – all state tax-free.

Property Tax Information

Nevada property taxes are relatively low compared to many states. The average effective property tax rate in Clark County is approximately 0.53% of assessed value. Plus, Nevada limits annual property tax increases to 3% for primary residences.
